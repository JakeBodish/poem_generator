
Sir Charles into my chamber coming inWhen I was writing of my ‘Fairy Queen’‘I pray’ said he ‘when Queen Mab you do seePresent my service to her MajestyAnd tell her I have heard Fames loud reportBoth of her beauty and her stately court ’When I Queen Mab within my fancy viewedMy thoughts bowed low fearing I should be rudeKissing her garment thin which fancy madeI knelt upon a thought like one that prayed And then in whispers soft I did presentHis humble service which in mirth was sentThus by imagination I have beenIn Fairy court and seen the Fairy Queen
