
They talk of short-lived pleasure–be it so–   Pain dies as quickly stern hard-featured pain Expires and lets her weary prisoner go    The fiercest agonies have shortest reign   And after dreams of horror comes again The welcome morning with its rays of peace    Oblivion softly wiping out the stainMakes the strong secret pangs of shame to ceaseRemorse is virtue’s root its fair increase   Are fruits of innocence and blessednessThus joy o’erborne and bound doth still release   His young limbs from the chains that round him press  Weep not that the world changes–did it keepA stable changeless state ’twere cause indeed to weep
