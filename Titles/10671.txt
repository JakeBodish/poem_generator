
Sweet shades why doe you seeke to give delight    To mee who deeme delight in this vilde place    Butt torment sorrow and mine owne disgrace    To taste of joy or your vaine pleasing sightShow them your pleasures who saw never night    Of greife wher joyings fauning smiling face    Appeers as day wher griefe found never space    Yett for a sigh a grone or envies spiteButt O on mee a world of woes doe ly    Or els on mee all harmes strive to rely    And to attend like servants bound to meeHeat in desire while frosts of care I prove    Wanting my love yett surfett doe with love    Burne and yett freeze better in hell to bee
