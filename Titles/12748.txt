
Lord with what care hast thou begirt us round      Parents first season us then schoolmasters      Deliver us to laws they send us boundTo rules of reason holy messengersPulpits and Sundays sorrow-dogging sin      Afflictions sorted anguish of all sizes      Fine nets and stratagems to catch us inBibles laid open millions of surprisesBlessings beforehand ties of gratefulness      The sound of glory ringing in our ears      Without our shame within our consciencesAngels and grace eternal hopes and fears       Yet all these fences and their whole array      One cunning bosom-sin blows quite away
