
 
Before the sixth day of the next new yearStrange wonders in this kingdom shall appearFour kings shall be assembled in this isleWhere they shall keep great tumult for awhile Many men then shall have an end of crossesAnd many likewise shall sustain great lossesMany that now full joyful are and gladShall at that time be sorrowful and sadFull many a Christians heart shall quake for fearThe dreadful sound of trump when he shall hear Dead bones shall then be tumbled up and downIn every city and in every town By day or night this tumult shall not ceaseUntil an herald shall proclaim a peaceAn herald strong the like was never bornWhose very beard is flesh and mouth is horn
