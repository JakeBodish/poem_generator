
I wage not any feud with Death          For changes wrought on form and face          No lower life that earths embrace May breed with him can fright my faith  Eternal process moving on          From state to state the spirit walks          And these are but the shatterd stalks Or ruind chrysalis of one  Nor blame I Death because he bare          The use of virtue out of earth          I know transplanted human worth Will bloom to profit otherwhere  For this alone on Death I wreak          The wrath that garners in my heart          He put our lives so far apart We cannot hear each other speak
