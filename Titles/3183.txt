
At first youll joy to see the playful snow    Like white moths trembling on the tropic air  Or waters of the hills that softly flow    Gracefully falling down a shining stair    And when the fields and streets are covered white    And the wind-worried void is chilly raw  Or underneath a spell of heat and light    The cheerless frozen spots begin to thaw   Like me youll long for home where birds glad song    Means flowering lanes and leas and spaces dry  And tender thoughts and feelings fine and strong    Beneath a vivid silver-flecked blue sky    But oh more than the changeless southern isles    When Spring has shed upon the earth her charm  Youll love the Northland wreathed in golden smiles    By the miraculous sun turned glad and warm
